When to seek emergency care (demo copy)

Sudden chest pain with shortness of sweat, sweating, or pain radiating to the arm or jaw; sudden weakness on one side of the body; trouble speaking; severe shortness of breath; loss of consciousness — these patterns often warrant immediate evaluation. This page is for UI demonstration only.

This tool does not replace emergency services. Not medical advice. Use local emergency numbers outside the US where applicable.
