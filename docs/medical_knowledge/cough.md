# Cough

Cough can come from viral illness, post-nasal drip, asthma, reflux, or infections. Duration and associated symptoms matter.

## What you can do now (general)
- Hydration and warm fluids may help.
- Honey can soothe cough for many adults (avoid in infants).
- Avoid smoking/vaping and other irritants.

## Seek urgent care now if
- Shortness of breath, chest pain, blue lips, confusion, or coughing up large amounts of blood.
- Severe wheezing or inability to speak full sentences.

## Follow-up questions to refine triage
- How long has the cough been present?
- Any fever, shortness of breath, chest pain, or wheezing?
- Dry vs productive (phlegm), and any blood?
