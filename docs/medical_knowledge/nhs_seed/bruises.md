# Bruises

## Overview
Bruising follows minor bumps and resolves over days to weeks. Colour changes are normal as the body clears trapped blood under the skin.

## What you can do now
- Cold pack soon after injury may limit swelling.
- Rest the area if painful.
- Note if bruising appears without clear injury.

## Red flags
- Large painless bruises with gum bleeding or fever.
- Bruising after head injury with headache or confusion.
- Rapidly expanding painful bruise after injury.

## When to seek medical advice
- Unexplained frequent bruising or bruising with fatigue.
- Bruising in someone on blood thinners after trauma — seek advice if extensive.
