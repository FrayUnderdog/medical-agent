# Dizziness and light-headedness

## Overview
“Dizzy” can mean spinning (vertigo), feeling faint, or imbalance. Causes range from benign inner-ear issues to dehydration and cardiovascular problems.

## What you can do now
- Sit or lie down if you feel faint to reduce fall risk.
- Rise slowly from lying or sitting.
- Hydration and regular meals if skipping food may contribute.

## Red flags
- Fainting with exertion, chest pain, palpitations, or new severe headache.
- Sudden trouble speaking, facial droop, or one-sided weakness.
- Continuous vertigo with new hearing loss or neurological signs.

## When to seek medical advice
- Episodes are frequent, prolonged, or affect driving or work safety.
- First severe vertigo episode without a clear benign trigger.
