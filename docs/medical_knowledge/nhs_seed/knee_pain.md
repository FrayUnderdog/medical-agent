# Knee pain

## Overview
Knee pain may follow overuse, mild twists, arthritis, or bursitis. Swelling, locking, and inability to bear weight are key warning signs.

## What you can do now
- Relative rest from aggravating activities.
- Ice for swelling flare-ups.
- Strengthen hips and quadriceps gently as pain allows.

## Red flags
- Hot swollen knee with fever — urgent assessment for joint infection.
- Severe deformity after injury or immediate large swelling after a pop.

## When to seek medical advice
- Locking where the knee cannot fully extend.
- Instability with recurrent giving way.
- Night pain and unexplained weight loss.
