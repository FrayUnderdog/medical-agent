# Neck pain

## Overview
Neck discomfort is often posture-related or after minor strain. Serious causes are uncommon but important to recognise.

## What you can do now
- Gentle range-of-motion within comfort, not forcing end range.
- Ergonomic screen height and breaks from prolonged looking down.
- Heat or cold trial for muscle spasm (whichever feels better).

## Red flags
- Fever with severe neck stiffness and confusion.
- Major trauma, midline tenderness after injury, or new arm weakness.
- Sudden severe headache with neck pain unlike prior episodes.

## When to seek medical advice
- Pain with progressive arm numbness or clumsiness.
- Symptoms after a significant accident even if pain was mild at first.
