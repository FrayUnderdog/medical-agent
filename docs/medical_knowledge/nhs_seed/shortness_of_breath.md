# Shortness of breath

## Overview
Breathlessness can reflect anxiety, asthma, infection, anaemia, clots, or heart problems. Severity and speed of onset guide urgency.

## What you can do now
- Sit upright; loosen tight clothing.
- Slow pursed-lip breathing if recommended for your known lung condition.
- Use your prescribed reliever inhaler if already diagnosed with asthma and it is your plan.

## Red flags
- Breathlessness at rest with chest pain, blue lips, confusion, or inability to speak in sentences.
- Sudden breathlessness after travel, surgery, or immobility.
- Wheeze with faintness or swelling of lips or tongue.

## When to seek medical advice
- New breathlessness that is progressive over days.
- Waking breathless needing extra pillows — clinician review.
