# Back pain

## Overview
Most back pain is mechanical and improves over days to weeks. Staying gently active often helps recovery more than strict bed rest.

## What you can do now
- Keep moving with comfortable walking and light daily activities.
- Heat packs may relax muscles for some people.
- Avoid heavy lifting and twisting until improving.

## Red flags
- Numbness between the legs, new bladder or bowel control problems, or severe leg weakness.
- Fever with back pain when infection risk is a concern.
- Major trauma, cancer history with new constant night pain, or unexplained weight loss.

## When to seek medical advice
- Pain not improving over several weeks or spreading down a leg with numbness.
- Pain waking you every night without positional relief.
