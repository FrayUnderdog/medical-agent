# COVID-like respiratory symptoms

## Overview
Fever, cough, sore throat, and fatigue can occur with many respiratory viruses. Testing policies vary by region and season; follow local public health guidance.

## What you can do now
- Rest, fluids, and monitoring symptoms.
- Isolate from others if you might be infectious, per local guidance.
- Seek testing advice through local services if available.

## Red flags
- Breathing difficulty at rest, chest pain, confusion, cyanosis, or collapse.
- Symptoms that escalate quickly in a person at higher risk.

## When to seek medical advice
- Symptoms persisting beyond expected recovery for you.
- New neurological symptoms, severe dehydration, or concern about pregnancy risk.
