# Hives (urticaria)

## Overview
Hives are raised itchy welts that can move around the body. They may be allergic, idiopathic, or triggered by heat, pressure, or infection.

## What you can do now
- Cool environment; loose clothing.
- Avoid known triggers when identified.
- Non-sedating antihistamines may help some adults if appropriate per label or clinician advice.

## Red flags
- Hives with lip or tongue swelling, wheeze, faintness, or rapid progression.
- Hives lasting beyond 24 hours leaving bruising (needs clinician review).

## When to seek medical advice
- Chronic hives most days for six weeks.
- Hives after a new drug — seek same-day advice.
