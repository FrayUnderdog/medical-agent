# Constipation

## Overview
Constipation means infrequent or hard stools. Diet, fluids, activity, and medicines commonly contribute.

## What you can do now
- Gradually increase dietary fibre if you usually tolerate it.
- Fluids and walking often help gut motility.
- Toilet routine: allow unhurried time after meals.

## Red flags
- Blood in stool with pain, unintended weight loss, or pencil-thin stools.
- Severe abdominal distension with vomiting.
- New constipation after age 50 with no clear lifestyle cause.

## When to seek medical advice
- Constipation lasting several weeks despite self-care.
- Needing laxatives very frequently — review underlying causes.
