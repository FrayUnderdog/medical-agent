# Severe abdominal pain

## Overview
Sudden severe belly pain can signal conditions needing urgent surgery or specialised care. Do not ignore rapid escalation.

## What you can do now
- If pain is extreme, spreading, with rigid abdomen or fainting — emergency care.
- Avoid strong painkillers that can mask examination findings until assessed if advised.

## Red flags
- Pain with copious blood in stool or vomit.
- Shoulder-tip pain with dizziness in relevant contexts.
- Pregnancy with severe pain or heavy bleeding.

## When to seek medical advice
- Localised severe pain worsening hour by hour.
- Fever with severe tenderness.
