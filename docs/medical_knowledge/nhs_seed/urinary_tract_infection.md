# Urinary tract infection (UTI) symptoms

## Overview
Burning urination, frequency, and urgency can suggest UTI but overlap with other conditions. Pregnancy and kidney infection risk change urgency.

## What you can do now
- Increase fluids if not contraindicated.
- Avoid irritants like caffeine and alcohol while symptomatic.
- Seek prompt testing and treatment advice rather than self-starting antibiotics without guidance.

## Red flags
- Fever, flank pain, vomiting, or rigors with urinary symptoms.
- Blood visible in urine with clots or inability to pass urine.
- Pregnancy with UTI symptoms — same-day advice.

## When to seek medical advice
- Symptoms beyond 48 hours without improvement.
- Recurrent UTIs needing prevention strategy.
