# Sore throat

## Overview
Throat pain is common with viral infections, dry air, voice strain, and occasionally bacterial infections. Most cases improve with simple care.

## What you can do now
- Warm drinks, cool drinks, or ice chips as preferred.
- Salt-water gargles may help some adults.
- Pain relief used according to over-the-counter label directions if appropriate for you.

## Red flags
- Drooling, inability to swallow saliva, muffled voice, or severe neck swelling.
- Trouble breathing or noisy breathing at rest.

## When to seek medical advice
- Severe pain with fever and no improvement over 48–72 hours.
- Recurrent sore throats that affect work or sleep.
