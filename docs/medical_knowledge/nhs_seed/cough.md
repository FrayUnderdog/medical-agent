# Cough

## Overview
Coughing clears the airways. It is common with colds, irritation, and some long-term conditions. The right follow-up depends on duration and associated symptoms.

## What you can do now
- Honey may soothe cough for adults (not for infants under one year).
- Hydration and humidified air can help.
- Avoid smoke exposure.

## Red flags
- Cough with trouble breathing, blue lips, or severe chest pain.
- Coughing blood or large amounts of coloured sputum with fever.
- Sudden choking sensation or suspected inhalation of an object.

## When to seek medical advice
- Cough lasting more than three weeks.
- Unexplained weight loss, night sweats, or coughing fits that disturb sleep persistently.
