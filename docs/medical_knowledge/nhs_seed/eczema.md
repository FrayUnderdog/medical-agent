# Eczema (atopic dermatitis)

## Overview
Eczema causes dry, itchy inflamed skin patches. Flares are common and often linked to triggers and skin-barrier stress.

## What you can do now
- Regular thick emollients, especially after bathing.
- Short lukewarm showers; pat dry and moisturise immediately.
- Cotton layers; avoid harsh soaps and prolonged damp gloves if irritating.

## Red flags
- Fever with rapidly spreading hot painful skin.
- Extensive weeping crusted lesions with systemic symptoms.
- Eye involvement with painful vision changes.

## When to seek medical advice
- Sleep severely affected most nights.
- Signs of infection: increasing pain, pus, spreading redness, fever.
