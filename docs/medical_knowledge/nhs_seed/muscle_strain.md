# Muscle strain

## Overview
Overstretching muscles during activity can cause pain and spasm. A gradual return to activity supports healing.

## What you can do now
- Brief rest then gentle movement within comfort.
- Ice or heat trial in the first days based on comfort.
- Avoid aggressive stretching in acute severe pain.

## Red flags
- A pop sensation with immediate severe weakness (possible tendon injury).
- Numbness, severe swelling, or fever after injury.

## When to seek medical advice
- Pain not improving over two to three weeks.
- Recurrent strains in the same muscle — review biomechanics.
