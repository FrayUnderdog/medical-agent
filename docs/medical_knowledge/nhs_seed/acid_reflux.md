# Acid reflux and heartburn

## Overview
Burning behind the breastbone after meals is common. Occasional symptoms may respond to lifestyle change; frequent symptoms deserve review.

## What you can do now
- Smaller meals; avoid lying flat for two to three hours after eating.
- Identify trigger foods (spicy, fatty, caffeine, alcohol) and moderate.
- Weight reduction if overweight may lessen symptoms over time.

## Red flags
- Pressure-like pain with shortness of breath, sweating, or pain spreading to arm or jaw — seek urgent assessment.
- Vomiting blood or black stools.
- Painful swallowing or food sticking.

## When to seek medical advice
- Symptoms most days for weeks.
- Night-time choking, unintended weight loss, or new symptoms after age 55.
