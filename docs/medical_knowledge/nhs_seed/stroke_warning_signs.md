# Stroke warning signs

## Overview
Stroke symptoms are sudden focal neurological deficits. Fast recognition saves time to treatment. This page is educational only.

## What you can do now
- Call emergency services immediately if face droop, arm weakness, or speech trouble is sudden.
- Note the time symptoms began; do not drive yourself.

## Red flags
- Sudden severe imbalance, double vision, or sudden worst headache with neurological signs.
- Transient symptoms that fully resolve still need urgent same-day assessment.

## When to seek medical advice
- Any sudden focal weakness or speech change — emergency.
- Sudden vision loss in one eye.
