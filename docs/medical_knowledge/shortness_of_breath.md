# Shortness of breath

Shortness of breath can be a medical emergency depending on severity and associated symptoms.

## Seek emergency care now if
- You can’t catch your breath, can’t speak full sentences, or symptoms are rapidly worsening.
- Shortness of breath with chest pain, blue lips, confusion, fainting, or coughing up blood.

## Follow-up questions to refine triage (if mild and stable)
- When did it start and is it getting worse?
- Any chest pain, fever, wheezing, or swelling in the legs?
- Any history of asthma/COPD, heart disease, or recent travel/surgery?
