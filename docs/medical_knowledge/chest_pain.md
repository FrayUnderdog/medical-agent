# Chest pain

Chest pain can have many causes, including heart/lung emergencies. In a chat setting, treat new or severe chest pain cautiously.

## Seek emergency care now if
- New chest pressure/pain, especially with shortness of breath, sweating, nausea, fainting, or pain radiating to arm/jaw/back.
- Chest pain with trouble breathing or coughing up blood.

## Follow-up questions to refine triage (if not emergent)
- When did it start and how severe is it (0–10)?
- Is it sharp vs pressure, and does it change with breathing or movement?
- Any shortness of breath, dizziness, or risk factors (age, smoking, known heart/lung disease)?
