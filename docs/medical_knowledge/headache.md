# Headache

Headaches are common and often benign, but some patterns can signal emergencies.

## What you can do now (general)
- Hydration and rest.
- Reduce screen strain and consider a calm, dark room.
- If you use pain relievers, follow the label and avoid exceeding daily maximums.

## Seek urgent care now if
- “Worst headache of your life,” sudden onset, or new neurologic symptoms (weakness, confusion, slurred speech).
- Headache with fever + stiff neck, fainting, or vision loss.

## Follow-up questions to refine triage
- Sudden vs gradual onset? Any fever or neck stiffness?
- Any vision changes, weakness, confusion, or head injury?
- History of migraines or similar headaches?
